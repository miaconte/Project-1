The After Party




Find events in your area!


Welcome to After Party, a place where you can find bands on tour, concert venues in your area, and After Party locations near your event.

How to use:


'Search by artist', type in your desired artist, their tour will appear if they're currently on the road!
'Search by Venue', use the name of the venue and the city separated by a comma, this will show any upcoming events the venue has.
Ex/ "Staples Center, Los Angeles".

'Create Saved Event', once you've searched for an event or a touring artist, you can click on the event you wish to remember and save it using this button.
'My Saved Events', this will store all of your upcoming tour date information that you wish to attend, venues, and after party locations you want to remember.

Map Feature, if you're looking for an event in an area unfamiliar to you, use our map! All of your choices will be marked with a pin.
